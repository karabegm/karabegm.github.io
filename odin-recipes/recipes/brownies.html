Fudge Brownies From Mt. Olympus

Description:

For years, I couldn’t seem to find a recipe for brownies that met my standards. Anytime I made homemade brownies, I wanted them to be just like the ones I ate as a kid – super fudgy, moist, and chocolaty, with crispy edges and crackly tops. But the thing is, these brownies didn’t come from a recipe. We always made them from a box. Nowadays, I prefer to bake from scratch, but no brownie recipe I tried could live up to the chocolate brownies of my childhood. So I set out on a mission to create the perfect recipe to give me the brownies I wanted.

Ingredients:

  • 5 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs and a yolk
  • tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/8 tsp baking soda
  • tsp salt
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:

Preheat oven to 325 degrees and grease a 8x8 inch pan
Melt the butter and mix with sugar, eggs, and vanilla
Stir in oil and cocoa powder
Fold in flour, baking soda, and salt mixture
Stir in chocolate chips. Then spread into lined baking pan
Bake for 30 minutes or until brownies are set. DO NOT OVERCOOK!
Once cooled, serve with ice cream and enjoy!
