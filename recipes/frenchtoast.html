French Toast

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • tsp Vanilla
  • tsp Cinnamon
  • Your choice of bread!
This french toast is delicious and only takes 15 minutes to make! Its recommended that you use quality bread, like Challah bread, but it's not required.

Directions:

  1. Mix together the eggs and milk
  2. Add in the sugar, vanilla, and cinnamon
  3. Preheat a non-stick pan with oil on a stove at medium high heat
  4. Dip both sides of the bread in the mixture and place it in the pan
  5. Flip over the bread as necessary till both sides are nicely toasted
  6. Top with maple syrup if you'd like
    7. SERVE & ENJOY! :)
